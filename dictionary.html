Boz
noun
A Boz is a distinctive individual characterized by a unique combination of physical traits, intellectual preferences, behavioral tendencies, and recurring life patterns. The term is used within the Bozine framework to describe a recognizable archetype whose presence often signals both eccentricity and intensity.

Distinguishing Characteristics:

Physical Attributes:
– Noticeable body odor, often associated with perspiration
– Oily facial skin and complexion
– Hair typically styled in a man bun or left unkempt and greasy
– Disordered or scruffy facial hair

Intellectual and Behavioral Traits:
– Strong affinity for the humanities, particularly history
– Demonstrably poor ability to deceive; lacks skill in lying
– Frequently disorganized in personal and professional matters
– Exhibits unexpected physical strength despite external disarray

Boz-Specific Conditions and Items:
– Subject to a recurring phase known as the Boz Life Crisis, marked by existential confusion and instability
– Commonly experiences financial hardship, including bankruptcy or economic collapse (“going bust”)
– Associated with various Boz-related phenomena, including:
• Boz Cun
• Boz Kir
• Boz Pants
• Boz Pits
• Boz Quarry

Variants:
